The process

Discovery

Once you have submitted a request to get started, we will get on a call to discuss your goals, needs, and preferences for your project. I'll ask questions to understand your business, target audience, and any existing challenges or pain points.

Proposal

After our initial discussion, I'll provide a clear and detailed proposal, including pricing and timelines. This will outline the scope of the project, including the deliverables, milestones, and deadlines. I'll also provide a breakdown of the costs involved, so you have a clear understanding of what to expect.

Creation

Once we've agreed on the details, I'll get to work on bringing your project to life. I will be in regular contact during this phase to keep you in the loop with progress, along with opportunities for feedback during each project milestone to ensure everything stays on track and that I deliver a final product that exceeds your expectations.

Launch

Once the final product has undergone rigorous assessment, it's time to launch your project and share it with the world! Most projects use native hosting options for their launch, but you can also export your site's code for external hosting. I'll be here to support you every step of the way, from the initial launch to providing ongoing maintenance and updates.
